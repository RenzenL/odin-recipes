Chicken Tandoori

Chicken Tandoori is an asian delicacy, known for its smoky and crispy outer layer and juicy insides with incredible spices. It is eaten with Chutney or Sauses.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Curd
  • Spices
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Prep the Chicken: Make 2–3 deep slits (scores) in the thickest parts of the chicken to help the marinade penetrate.
  2. Marinate: Cover and refrigerate for at least 6 hours, preferably overnight (up to 24 hours) for the best flavor and tenderness.
  3. Preheat the oven to 240°C (470°F). Place chicken on a wire rack over a foil-lined tray. Roast for 25–30 minutes, basting with butter/oil halfway through. Broil for the last 3–5 minutes for charring.
