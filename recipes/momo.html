Momo

Momo is a Nepali delicacy. It is a nepalese version of dumpling made from vegetables and grounded meat.

  • Grounded Chicken Meat 500grms

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Prepare the filling
  3. Cover the dough with damp cloth
  4. Roll the dough and fill it
  5. Shape the dough
  6. Steam the momos
